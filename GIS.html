Vancouver Green Ways, Shelters & Government Housing, 2021:
The purpose of this map is to see if there are more or less green ways near homeless shelters and government owned housing buildings. That is, the map is aiming to see if there are more or less greenways available in areas surrounding low-income housing and homeless shelters versus other areas in the city. All data layers were downloaded from the Vancouver Open Data Portal website. The interactive map can be found here.

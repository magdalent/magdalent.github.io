Finding the least coast path of northern resident killer whales

Why
The goal of this project was to calculate the least cost migratory route of northern resident killer whale populations from the western edge of strait of Juan de Fuca to Langara Island within the coastal waters of British Columbia. Considering the spatial distribution and relative risk of fishing vessel traffic, merchant (bulk/cargo) vessel traffic, and exploratory well sites.

What
This project was completed for Geography 328 (GIS Analysis) at the University of Victoria

How
The project was made with QGIS and open source data sets
